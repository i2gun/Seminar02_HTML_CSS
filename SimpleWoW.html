Choosing Your Race

World of Warcraft offers a variety of races, each with its own unique characteristics and lore. Choose your race wisely as it will impact your gameplay experience.

  • Humans: Versatile and adaptable.
  • Orcs: Strong and resilient warriors.
  • Elves: Graceful and agile, with a connection to nature.
  • Undead: Cunning and resistant to necromantic magic.

Picking Your Class

Each class in World of Warcraft comes with its own unique abilities and playstyles. Consider your preferred playstyle when choosing a class.

  • Warrior: Master of melee combat.
  • Mage: Harnesses the power of arcane magic.
  • Rogue: Stealthy and deadly.
  • Priest: Healer or damage dealer with holy magic.

Race and Class Combinations

Certain races have racial abilities that complement specific classes. Here are some popular combinations:

  • Human Warrior: Adaptable in both PvE and PvP.
  • Orc Warlock: Powerful combination for damage dealing.
  • Night Elf Hunter: Stealthy and attuned to nature.
  • Undead Priest: Resistant to fear effects, ideal for healing.
